📊 Annual Sales Performance Report Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Annual Sales Performance Report Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to analyze overall business sales performance across multiple dimensions, including revenue, profit, regions, product categories, sales representatives, customer segments, and products. The dashboard provides decision-makers with a comprehensive view of sales trends and business performance through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters.

🎯 Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to:

Monitor annual sales, revenue, and profit performance.
Compare sales across different regions and product categories.
Identify top-performing sales representatives.
Analyze customer segments and purchasing behavior.
Evaluate product-wise sales contribution.
Support business decisions through interactive reporting.
📊 Dashboard Features
Interactive Region, Category, and Customer Type slicers
KPI Cards for Total Revenue and Total Profit
Region-wise Sales Analysis
Category-wise Sales Distribution
Top Performing Sales Representatives
Customer Type Revenue Analysis
Place-wise Revenue Trend
Annual Sales Trend
Product-wise Sales Performance
Dynamic charts that update instantly based on selected filters

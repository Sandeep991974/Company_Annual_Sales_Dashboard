# 📊 Annual Sales Performance Report Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Annual Sales Performance Report Dashboard** is an interactive **Microsoft Excel** dashboard designed to analyze business sales performance across multiple dimensions, including revenue, profit, regions, product categories, sales representatives, customer segments, and products. It provides valuable insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI cards, and interactive filters, enabling data-driven business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Objective

* Monitor annual sales, revenue, and profit performance.
* Compare sales across different regions and product categories.
* Identify top-performing sales representatives.
* Analyze customer segments and purchasing behavior.
* Evaluate product-wise sales contribution.
* Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

* Interactive Region, Category & Customer Type Slicers
* Dynamic KPI Cards for Revenue & Profit
* Region-wise Sales Analysis
* Category-wise Sales Distribution
* Top Performing Sales Representatives
* Customer Type Revenue Analysis
* Place-wise Revenue Trend
* Annual Sales Trend
* Product-wise Sales Performance
* Fully Interactive Charts & Visualizations

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 💰 Total Revenue
* 📈 Total Profit
* 🌍 Region-wise Sales
* 📦 Category-wise Sales
* 👨‍💼 Top Sales Representatives
* 👥 Customer Type Analysis
* 🛍️ Product-wise Sales Performance
* 📅 Annual Sales Trend
